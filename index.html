EDGE CITY // ASSASSIN

Welcome to Assassin. There are two goals of this game: be the last one standing and get out as many people as possible. On Day 1, you will receive a target. To get out your target, you need to tap them with three fingers on their wristband. If they aren't wearing a wristband, they should be, and you can tap them on their wrist. If you succeed in getting out your target, then your target's target becomes your target. (Mwah ha ha) The cycle continues until only one person (the Victor) remains. Each day, there will be a new defense mechanism for staying in the game, which we will announce in a Telegram chat that all players will be added to. So, if someone taps your wristband, but you have your defense mechanism in place, then their tap is neutralized.

To enter the game, fill out the sign-up form here.

All players must join the Telegram group chat — this is where daily defense mechanisms and announcements will be posted.

IMPORTANT RULE: No getting someone out during an active session and no sprinting on the streets of Healdsburg.

Let the mischief commence.

If you have any questions, reach out to @akos5111 and @juliamrobbins.

Ground Rules · Field Protocol
01

The Touch — three fingers, one bracelet.

A Mark is neutralized only when their assassin lays a three-finger touch on the target's Edge City bracelet. No water guns, no spoons, no proxies — the bracelet touch is the single canonical kill. Land it, then log it here to inherit your next Mark.

02

Log it honestly.

The eliminator self-reports the valid kill here immediately. The system reassigns your next Mark and broadcasts the hit to the whole village. The admin can reverse a mistaken or disputed entry.

03

Daily Directives — the rules keep changing.

Every day, fresh field directives drop from command: new ways to stay safe or get burned (a passphrase you must answer, a zone that's off-limits, a gesture that grants immunity). They appear pinned at the top of this page and on your dashboard, and ping every agent the instant they're issued. Miss one and you're exposed — check back daily.
